Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 bulb garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorn
  • 1 cup water

Steps:

  1. Marinate the meat in soy sauce and garlic for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the meat until slightly browned.
  3. Add vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorn. Bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer for 40 minutes or until meat is tender.
  5. Serve with steamed rice.
