Leche Flan

Ingredients:

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Caramelize sugar in a llanera or baking pan. Set aside.
  2. Mix egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla.
  3. Strain the mixture, then pour it into the caramelized pan.
  4. Steam for 30 minutes or until firm.
  5. Chill before serving.
