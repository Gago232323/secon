Pancit

Ingredients:

  • 500g rice noodles
  • 200g chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup shrimp
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Soak noodles in water until softened, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion.
  3. Add chicken and shrimp, then cook until done.
  4. Add carrots and cabbage, and stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  5. Add noodles and soy sauce. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.
