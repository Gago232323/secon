Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly or ribs
  • 1 liter water
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 pack tamarind soup mix
  • 1 cup radish, sliced
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2 green chili peppers

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pot and add pork, onion, and tomatoes. Cook until tender.
  2. Add the tamarind soup mix and stir well.
  3. Add radish and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add kangkong and green chili peppers. Simmer for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
